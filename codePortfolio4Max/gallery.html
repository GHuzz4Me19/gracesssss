Japanese Shrine

The ceramics peice I constructed in highschool for my grade 11 final assignment. I wanted to do somthing bigger then normal but made it very difficult for the kiln to process. it can 1) either come out completely fine. 2) crack and break. or 3) most commonly with peices this big it can expload and break all the other peices in it. It actually cracked in the kilnl becuasse it was too big. but my teacher trusted me to do sucha big peice but there was a lot of presure on me not to destroy the peices people had been working on for the whole semester.... before the kiln you have to let it dry enough to the point its completely solid after sculpting. a trick is to put it in the same room as the kiln to make it dry faster. but if it dries too fast it can crack that way too.
